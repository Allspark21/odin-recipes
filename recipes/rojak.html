Rojak

Description

Rujak or rojak is a salad dish of Javanese origin, commonly found in Indonesia, Malaysia and Singapore.

Ingredient

  • Fruits
  • Nuts
  • Black Sauce

Steps

  1. Remove skin from jicama, mangoes and pineapple if necessary.
  2. Top with peanuts and sesame seeds.
  3. Serve immediately with a fork or bamboo skewer sticks.
